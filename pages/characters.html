Main characters

Meet the heroes

Wednesday Addams

The titular main protagonist of Tim Burton's Netflix series, Wednesday. She is the daughter of Morticia Addams and Gomez Addams, the older sister of Pugsley Addams, the niece of Uncle Fester and Ophelia Frump, and the granddaughter of Hester Frump.

Wednesday Thing

Thing is a tritagonist in series Wednesday. He is a sentient disembodied hand and friend of Wednesday Addams, who is sent by Morticia Addams and Gomez Addams to watch over her at Nevermore Academy.

Enid Sinclair

Enid Sinclair is one of the deuteragonists in series Wednesday. Enid is a werewolf student of Nevermore Academy, who is the roommate and best friend of Wednesday Addams. She is Wednesday's sunny and colorful Californian roommate at Nevermore Academy. Enid hails from a pack of San Francisco werewolves.

Tyler Galpin

Tyler Galpin is the secondary antagonist in series, Wednesday. He is son of Sheriff Donovan Galpin and his late wife Francoise, and works as a part-time barista at the Weathervane, the local coffee shop in Jericho, Vermont. Like his mother, Tyler is a Hyde who forcibly falls under Laurel Gates's control to kill supernatural beings known as "outcasts".

Gomez Addams

Gomez Addams is a minor character in series Wednesday. He is the husband of Morticia Addams, younger brother of Uncle Fester, father of Wednesday and Pugsley Addams, and the son-in-law of Hester Frump.

Morticia Addams

Morticia Addams (née Frump) is a minor character in series, Wednesday. She is the wife of Gomez Addams, mother of Wednesday and Pugsley Addams, the sister-in-law of Uncle Fester, the daughter of Hester Frump, and the older sister of Ophelia Frump.

Pugsley Addams

Pugsley Addams is a minor character in Wednesday. He is the son of Gomez and Morticia Addams, the younger brother of Wednesday Addams, the nephew of Uncle Fester and Ophelia Frump, and the grandson of Hester Frump.

Hester Frump

Hester Frump is a supporting character in series Wednesday. She is the mother of Morticia Addams and Ophelia Frump, the mother-in-law of Gomez Addams, and the maternal grandmother of Wednesday and Pugsley Addams.

Marylin Thornhill

Laurel Gates, also known as Marylin Thornhill, is the main antagonist in series Wednesday. She and her family were the ancestors of early 17th century Pilgrim and Jericho founder Joseph Crackstone and were born into great wealth.

Barry Dort

Barry Dort is a major antagonist in the second season of Wednesday. He is the new principal of Nevermore Academy and the successor of Larissa Weems.

Larissa Weems

Larissa Weems is a main character in series Wednesday. She is a shapeshifter and the principal of Nevermore Academy, and a former student who was roommates with Morticia Addams when they attended the academy together.

Xavier Thorpe

Xavier Thorpe is one of the deuteragonists in series Wednesday. He is a psychic student at Nevermore Academy who is the childhood friend of Wednesday Addams and the the ex-boyfriend of Bianca Barclay. He is the leader of the team, the 'Amonti Llado', in the Poe Cup, alongside his teammate Ajax Petropolus.

Ajax Petropolus

Ajax Petropolus is one of the supporting characters in series Wednesday. He is a gorgon student of Nevermore Academy and the ex-boyfriend of Enid Sinclair. Awkward and shy, Ajax is anxious about looking anyone in the eye. He is a member of the Nightshade Society and is on Xavier Thorpe's team, the 'Amontillado', in the Poe Cup.

Bianca Barclay

Bianca Barclay is a former antagonist turned supporting character in series Wednesday. She is a siren who attends Nevermore Academy while being the ex-girlfriend of Xavier Thorpe and a friendly rival to Wednesday Addams.

Isadora Capri

Isadora Capri is a supporting character in series Wednesday. She is Nevermore Academy's new head teacher of music.

Lucas Walker

Lucas Walker is a recurring antagonist turned anti-hero in series Wednesday. He is the son of Mayor Noble Walker and Lou-Anne Walker.

Isaac Night

Isaac Night, also known as Slurp, is one of the main antagonists in series Wednesday. He is the brother of Francoise Galpin, the maternal uncle of Tyler Galpin, and the brother-in-law of Donovan Galpin. After being killed in a experiment, Isaac was accidentally brought back from the dead as a zombie by Pugsley Addams.

Agnes DeMille

Agnes DeMille is the tritagonist in the second season of Wednesday. She is a student of Nevermore Academy who assists her idol, Wednesday Addams in an investigation to save her friend, Enid Sinclair.
