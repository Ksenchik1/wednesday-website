Chilling Adventures of Sabrina

Release date: 2018-2020

Number of seasons: 4, completed

Country: USA

Sabrina Spellman is no ordinary teenager. She's a half-witch living with her witch aunts, Hilda and Zelda. On her sixteenth birthday, she must renounce her mortal life, enroll in a witch academy, and swear allegiance to a dark god. Sabrina isn't happy with this option—she doesn't want to lose touch with her friends and boyfriend, and mortal life is too good. She manages to achieve a small victory , attending both the witch academy and a regular school, remaining close to her loved ones, but the servants of darkness never give up hope of swaying her. Now Sabrina must defend her right to live the life she wants, not the life of those around her.

Fate: The Winx Saga

Release date: 2021-2022

Number of seasons: 2, completed

Country: UK, Italy

An adaptation of a familiar animated series from childhood. Five magical girls from different worlds enroll in Alfea Academy to hone their magical powers. One of them, Bloom, wields the power of fire and grew up among humans, yet her strength is incredibly high. The balance between the forces of light and darkness depends on how she uses her abilities. During her training and meeting new friends, Bloom learns that Earth is not her home world , and that she was abandoned to her parents. Now, she wants to uncover the secret of her origin, unaware that it will also reveal the terrible lie she has lived with for sixteen years.

Lock and key

Release date: 2020-2022

Number of seasons: 3, completed

Country: USA, Canada

The Locke family has lost their father, Randall, and after the funeral, they move into their father's old house. Widow Nina and her children, Tyler, Kinsey, and Bode, begin to realize that the mansion's name, " Key House ," is not for nothing. Many of the doors are magical, and the keys that fit them can grant magical powers. Each key can either bless or attract something terrible that seeks to use this power for its own benefit. As the family learns the secrets of the keys, they explore magic and the world they've seen change , now filled with demons, the possessed, and a danger to all of humanity. The number of keys is growing, making the likelihood of them falling into the wrong hands even greater.

Very strange things

Release date: 2016 – present

Number of seasons: 4, season 5 is in development

Country: USA

In the small town of Hawkins, Indiana, research is being conducted in a mysterious laboratory—it's said to be for industrial purposes, but in reality, it's used to study paranormal phenomena . One of the test subjects escapes just after a child in town is abducted by a creature from the other world. The girl, named Eleven by new acquaintances, joins a group of teenagers conducting their own investigation. They, too, have suffered to some degree from the monsters from the Upside Down and are now trying to bring their friend's brother home. Along the way, they face the usual teenage problems.

The Umbrella Academy

Release date: 2019 – present

Number of seasons: 3, season 4 is in development

Country: USA

One day, several dozen women with no signs of pregnancy become mothers. Seven of these unusual children are taken in by billionaire Reginald Hargreeves and raised as his own. He creates the Umbrella Academy for them , so they can learn to control their supernatural powers and, in the future, protect the world from destruction. The grown children scatter across the world, but are destined to reunite at their adoptive father's funeral. During the reunion, the entire group discovers the ability to travel through time, and Five delivers terrifying news: in a few days, their world will end. Now the family must prevent the apocalypse.
